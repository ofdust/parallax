A Day in the Life of Pluto

This is Pluto. He's a dwarf planet that lives 30-49 AU away from the sun.

Pluto likes to hang out with his friends Neptune and Uranus.

Sometimes some of the other planets are mean to him because he's a dwarf planet now.

But it's okay; Pluto still has a big heart.
